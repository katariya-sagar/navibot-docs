Product vision

Make navigation explainable, adaptive, and useful.

NaviBot is designed as a conversational navigation layer that helps users understand where to go, why a route is suggested, what trade-offs exist, and what to do next.

⌁

Context-aware guidance

Use route, time, constraints, and user intent to provide better navigation choices.

◇

Explainable decisions

Show why a route is recommended: speed, safety, cost, distance, or convenience.

↗

Journey planning

Support multi-step travel, handoffs, reminders, and route alternatives.

▣

Architecture

Simple MVP architecture that can scale later.

The initial architecture separates user input, routing/context services, decision logic, response generation, and feedback loops. This keeps the product testable while leaving room for map providers, AI services, and integrations.

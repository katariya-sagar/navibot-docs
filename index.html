Product overview

A conversational layer for route planning, context, and decision support.

NaviBot helps users move from vague intent to confident action. Instead of only showing a map, it explains options, trade-offs, timing, constraints, and next steps in plain language.

For travelers

Ask natural questions, compare options, and get guidance that adapts to the journey.

For builders

Document the system boundaries, interfaces, and quality bar before implementation expands.

For stakeholders

Track scope, milestones, risks, and the product narrative from MVP through later releases.

Architecture

Simple MVP architecture with room to scale.

The first version separates user input, intent parsing, route and context services, recommendation logic, response generation, and feedback. That makes the product easier to test, explain, and extend with real map providers or AI services later.

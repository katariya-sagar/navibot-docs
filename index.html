Product overview

Route planning that explains the decision, not just the direction.

NaviBot is a conversational layer above routing data. It interprets a trip request, weighs constraints, compares options, and returns a recommendation with the reasons behind it.

01

Understand intent

Capture destination, timing, route preferences, constraints, and uncertainty in one structured request.

02

Compare options

Rank candidates by travel time, reliability, cost, transfer complexity, and walking effort.

03

Explain next steps

Return a clear recommendation, alternatives, trade-offs, and the next action the user should take.

Architecture

Separated layers keep the MVP testable and the future flexible.

NaviBot keeps the client experience, intent parsing, route context, scoring, response generation, and feedback loop distinct. That makes provider changes easier and keeps recommendations explainable.
